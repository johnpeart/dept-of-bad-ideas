The Department of Bad Ideas is a podcast about policy, disconnected from reality.

In each episode, two guests engage in a head-to-head battle of unconventional policy wits. We ask them to solve real problems in the most unsaleable, impractical and impossible ways.

There are 3 rounds:

  1. Unsaleable Policy Division

    Our guests are given a real policy problem, and they're asked to come up with a working policy solution. But there's a catch: it has to be completely unsellable. It has to be so good that any minister would recoil in horror at the very idea of fixing that problem with that particular solution.

    Points are awarded for presentation, practicality and unsaleability.

  2. Impossible Implementation Unit

    Next, our guests are given another, real issue to solve; but this time things are different: the solution has to be electoral gold, but entirely impossible to implement.

    Points are awarded for presentation, practicality and saleability.

  3. In The Thick Of It

    The final round is a quick fire round, inspired by the hit radio show, Just a Minute. We'll give our guests a policy problem, and they have to describe a solution to it, on the fly, for a minute without repetition, hesitation or deviation. If they falter, their opponent takes over and the clock keeps ticking.

    Points are awarded to who ever is still talking at the end of the minute.

Who will win? Tune in and find out.
