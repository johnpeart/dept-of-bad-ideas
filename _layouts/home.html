The Department of Bad Ideas is a podcast about policy, disconnected from reality.

In each episode, two guests engage in a head-to-head battle of unconventional policy wits; solving real world problems in the most impractical and impossible ways.

Listen now

Each episode of the Department of Bad Ideas consists of three rounds.

Unsaleable Policy Division

In round one, guests are given a real policy problem, and they're asked to come up with a working policy solution. But there's a catch: it has to be completely unsellable. It has to be so good that any minister would recoil in horror at the very idea of fixing that problem with that particular solution.

Points are awarded for presentation, practicality and unsaleability.

Impossible Implementation Unit

In the second round, our guests are given another real issue to solve, but this time things are different: the solution has to be electoral gold, but entirely impossible to implement.

Points are awarded for presentation, practicality and saleability.

In The Thick Of It

The final round is a quick fire round, inspired by the hit radio show, Just a Minute. We'll give our guests a policy problem, and they have to describe a solution to it, on the fly, for a minute without repetition, hesitation or deviation. If they falter, their opponent takes over and the clock keeps ticking.

Points are awarded to who ever is still talking at the end of the minute.

Who will win? Tune in and find out.
